Next Notes

A new Nextcloud app for an enhanced organization of notes.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Installation

Install the app from the Nextcloud Appstore or download the git release and place the content in owncloud/apps/ or nextcloud/apps/.

Basic usage

The Next Notes app integrates within the app menu. It gives you the opportunity to organize your personal notes, tag it with necessary catchphrases and search for them. After you installed the app, you have to enable it in the apps menu. Following this, you can use the Next Notes app by choosing it in the Nextcloud menu. The usage is pretty simple. Just write down what comes up to your mind into the editor field and don't forget to click on safe in the editor toolbar. That's it. Optionally you could tag your note for better search possibilities later, but you don't have to. It's up to you.

Search

You have 3 possibilities to search (example usage in the screenshots)

  • Fulltext search: Search for notes by just entering your query into the search field.
  • Tag search: Search for your tags by entering your query enclosed by hashtags. Example: '#search phrase#'
  • Combine it: Example: 'term #tag# #tag2# term2'

Screenshots and example usage

The Next Notes app gives you much more possibilities for your personal organization. With Next Notes you will be able to create notes with a simple markup for enhanced usability. It gives you the opportunity to write down small hasty notes, like a recall you mustn't forget. But it also allows you to create great stories or large texts, that you don't want to loose. Next Notes also adopts the theme you set up for your Nextcloud. Moreover it lets you tag your notes, in order to find them much quicker, if you already created a bunch of notes.
